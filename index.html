FOLLOW THE MONEY — HOW CAPITAL FLOWS THROUGH THE FINANCIAL SYSTEM

How Money Actually
Moves in Markets

At every step, someone extracts value. This is how the system works.

1
💰 Savers & Investors
Individuals, pension funds, sovereign wealth funds deposit and invest capital seeking returns
Capital source
Broker routes order · earns commission + spread
2
🏢 Brokers & Market Makers
Route orders to exchanges or act as counterparty. Market makers earn the bid-ask spread on every trade
Spread + commission
Clearing house guarantees settlement
3
🏛 Exchanges, Clearing & Custody
Exchange matches orders. Clearing house (DTCC, LCH) guarantees both sides. Custodian holds final title
Listing + clearing fees
Investment banks underwrite new issuances
4
🏦 Investment Banks (Primary Market)
Price and underwrite IPOs, bonds, M&A. Distribute to institutional investors. The only point where company gets money
1–7% underwriting fee
Capital flows to productive use
5
🏭 Companies & Governments
Receive capital to build, hire, expand. Repay via dividends, bond coupons, and future earnings growth
Capital deployed
💡

The Key Insight: The secondary market (where most trading happens) sends zero money to the original company. Only the PRIMARY market (IPO, bond issuance) puts capital directly into corporate hands. Every other trade simply moves ownership between investors — while intermediaries extract small fees at massive scale.

The Core Trade-off: Buying options gives you positive gamma (profit from big moves) but negative theta (pay daily time decay). Selling options gives you positive theta (collect daily premium) but negative gamma (dangerous if wrong). You CANNOT have both — this is the fundamental tension every options trader must manage daily.

📈 Long Call
Strongly Bullish
Buy call option. Max profit: unlimited. Max loss: premium paid only.
📊 Covered Call
Neutral → Mildly Bullish
Own stock + sell call. Generate monthly income on shares you hold.
🦅 Iron Condor
Neutral — Range Bound
Sell OTM call + put spreads. Profit when stock stays in range. 30–45 DTE.
🎭 Long Straddle
Big Move — Any Direction
Buy ATM call + put. Best for earnings plays when direction is unknown.
🛡️ Bull Call Spread
Moderately Bullish
Buy lower call + sell higher call. Cheaper than long call. Defined risk.
🔮 Cash-Secured Put
Mildly Bullish
Sell put + hold cash. Get paid to wait to buy stock at your target price.
